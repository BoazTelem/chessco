## 1. Scope

This DPA forms part of the Order Form between the parties and applies whenever Chessco processes personal data on behalf of the Controller in connection with the services described in the Order Form (e.g. coach accounts managing multiple students).

## 2. Subject matter and duration

- **Subject matter**: provision of the Chessco platform features described in the Order Form.
- **Duration**: same as the Order Form.
- **Nature and purpose**: see Privacy Policy.
- **Type of personal data**: account data, game data, marketplace activity (as defined in the Privacy Policy).
- **Categories of data subjects**: Controller's authorized users (e.g. students enrolled by a coach).

## 3. Roles

Chessco is the **Processor**. The customer is the **Controller**. Each party is independently responsible for compliance with applicable data-protection law in its role.

## 4. Processor obligations

Chessco shall:

1. Process personal data only on documented instructions from the Controller, except as required by EU or member state law.
2. Ensure persons authorized to process the personal data are bound by confidentiality.
3. Implement appropriate technical and organizational measures (Annex A).
4. Engage Sub-processors only as listed (Annex B) and notify the Controller of additions with 30 days notice.
5. Assist the Controller with data-subject requests, DPIAs, and supervisory authority consultations.
6. Delete or return personal data on termination of the Order Form, subject to retention obligations under law (e.g. financial records).
7. Make available all information necessary to demonstrate compliance and submit to reasonable audits.

## 5. Controller obligations

The Controller shall:

1. Provide lawful instructions for processing.
2. Ensure it has a lawful basis to share personal data with Chessco.
3. Inform its data subjects of the processing as required.

## 6. Sub-processors

See Privacy Policy "Who we share with". Chessco maintains the up-to-date list at {SUBPROCESSORS_URL}. The Controller may object to a new Sub-processor within 14 days of notice; if Chessco cannot find a reasonable alternative, the Controller may terminate the affected services.

## 7. International transfers

Where personal data is transferred outside the EU/EEA or UK, Chessco relies on the EU Standard Contractual Clauses (Module Two) and, for UK data, the UK International Data Transfer Addendum, in each case incorporated by reference into this DPA.

## 8. Personal data breach

Chessco notifies the Controller without undue delay and within 72 hours of becoming aware of a personal data breach. Notification includes the nature of the breach, categories and approximate numbers of data subjects, likely consequences, and measures taken or proposed.

## 9. Liability

Liability under this DPA is subject to the limitations in the Order Form.
